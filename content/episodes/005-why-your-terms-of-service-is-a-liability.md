Let me guess how your terms of service was written: someone on your team Googled "terms of service template," found one that looked official, changed the company name, and called it done.

I've seen this exact scenario hundreds of times. And every single time, the terms of service that was supposed to protect the business is actually creating liability. This episode breaks down why, and what a terms of service actually needs to do in 2026.
